After traveling back in time, Geek and Goth have come across Robin Hood. It seems that Robin and his band of theives (calling themselves the "Merry Men") have just pulled off their first heist. Robin convinces the Merry Men that he will catch up to them later with the loot -- once he makes sure they weren't followed... Robin Hood has other plans though.

He is finally rich. There is more money in that chest than he has ever seen and now his plans are to live a life of luxery. Screw the poor and screw the band of Merry Men. He is going to go far far away, where nobody can find him. The only place to do that is in Montana... after all, isn't it known for its wide open spaces? It is up to Geek and Goth to convince him to give his loot to the poor and be a hero instead of a vilian. Robin Hood agrees, but on the lone condition that they beat him in an archery contest.
